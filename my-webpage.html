Burgers Park

  • Burgers Park is a public park located in the central business district of Pretoria, South Africa. It was established in 1892 and was named after Thomas Francois Burgers, a former president of the South African Republic.
  • The park covers an area of 4.2 hectares and is known for its beautiful gardens and a variety of trees, including jacarandas, plane trees, and palm trees. The park also features several fountains and ponds, as well as a bandstand.
  • Burgers Park is a popular destination for picnics and leisurely walks. It is also home to a number of bird species, including Egyptian geese, ducks, and swans. Additionally, the park has a rich history and is a popular spot for cultural events and performances.

Union Buildings

  • The Union Buildings are located in Pretoria, South Africa, and serve as the official seat of the South African government. They were designed by Sir Herbert Baker and completed in 1913.
  • The Union Buildings are famous for their distinctive sandstone architecture and the 9-meter-tall bronze statue of Nelson Mandela, which was unveiled on the lawns in front of the buildings in 2013.
  • The Union Buildings also feature beautiful gardens and terraces, which offer stunning views of the city. The gardens are home to a variety of indigenous plants, including a number of protea species, and are a popular destination for both tourists and locals alike.

SANDF Wall of Rememberance

  • The South African National Defence Force Wall of Remembrance is a memorial structure dedicated to fallen soldiers who died in the line of duty during military operations since 1961.
  • The wall is located in Thaba Tshwane, Pretoria and was officially opened on the 4th of October 2016 by the former President of South Africa, Jacob Zuma.
  • The Wall of Remembrance is designed as a curved wall with 4,048 bronze plaques, each containing the name, rank, and date of death of a fallen soldier. The wall is 80 meters long and 3 meters high, and it also includes a central feature, which is a bronze statue of a soldier in full battle gear, holding a gun and standing at attention.
